Help Close the Funding Gap

Efficiency should not be mistaken for sufficiency. Valuable programs and resources have to be excluded or privately funded in order to best serve our students. With equitable state funding, charter schools like Renaissance Academy can better support students with quality education, wrap-around support systems, and pathways to greater opportunities.
