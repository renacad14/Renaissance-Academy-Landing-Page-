3 |Inequitable Funding

We do more, with less.

We educate each student for $17,272 — less than half of what the Rochester City School District spends per pupil. We run lean, and it shows: our students consistently outperform their peers.

Charter schools like RA receive no facilities support and less public funding overall. Each year, we rely on local contributions to sustain essential programs — social-emotional support, summer learning, and before-school care.
